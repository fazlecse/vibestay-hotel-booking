Friendly Real Estate: New opportunities for renters and homeowners

  • January, 15 2025
  • By Admin
  • 5 Min Read

With the help of founding partner Greystar, we have brought AFA to the UK as part of our global expansion of Airbnb-friendly apartments after our success in the US. There are now around 1,500 Airbnb-friendly flats available for renters at Sailmakers in Canary Wharf, Greenford Quay in West London, and Fizzy Walthamstow in East London.

"Given the success of our partnership in the US, we're delighted to help introduce Airbnb-friendly Apartments to the UK," stated Maria Hatch, Senior Director, Client Relations at Greystar. Residents benefit from increased financial flexibility, and our community management teams can keep an eye on hosting activities to make sure everything goes without a hitch and doesn't affect the larger community.

An update on VibeStay-friendly Apartments in the US

As it enters its third year, AFA has grown despite the fact that mortgage rates and housing costs in the US are still very high. With the recent addition of new partners like Geolo Capital, Roundhouse, and Villa Serena Communities, the program currently includes over 750 structures with over 200,000 units spread across the US. Currently, 14 of the top 50 National Multifamily Housing Council (NMHC) Managers and 12 of the top 50 NMHC Owners have Airbnb-friendly apartments.

VibeStay-friendly Condos

Despite the fact that housing costs and mortgage rates remain extremely high in the United States, AFA has expanded as it begins its third year. The initiative now has approximately 750 structures with over 200,000 units dispersed around the United States, thanks to the recent inclusion of new partners like Geolo Capital, Roundhouse, and Villa Serena Communities. Airbnb-friendly units are currently available in 12 of the top 50 National Multifamily Housing Council (NMHC) Owners and 14 of the top 50 NMHC Managers.

AFA offers renters a fantastic opportunity to earn money through Airbnb while they are overseas, as many of them are still having financial difficulties. AFA's steady growth and its partners' aspirations to expand internationally are examples of the model's adaptability. I'm eager to see it develop.
